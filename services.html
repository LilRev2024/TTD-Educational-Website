The Services we offer


TDT Academy focuses on Science and Art. We will be giving Students a better understanding of what Science and Art is all about.

We are all about making learning fun. There are so many challenging topics under Science and Art, but not to worry, we will make this journey an exciting and fun one.

The Science Syllabus will be focusing on the following:
  • What is Science?
  • The three branches of Science
  • What is Natural Science and its components, such as Biology, Geology, Astronomy, Oceanography, Chemistry, and Meteorology.
  • What is Social Science and its components, such as Sociology and Psychology.
  • What is Format Science and its components, such as Mathematics, Computer Science, and Artical Intelligence.
The Art Syllabus will be focusing on the following:
  • What is Music and the different generes of Music, for example, Jazz, Pop, Rock, Rhythm and Blues, Regga, and Hip Hop
  • What is Sculpture and the seven elements such as, line, color, value, shape, form, space, and texture.
  • What is Drawing and the six main techniques in drawing, such as, Sighting / Measuring, The Grid Technique, Triangular Grid Technique, Linear Perspective, Basic Facial Proportions, and Figure Proportions and Gesture Drawing.
